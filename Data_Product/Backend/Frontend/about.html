Our Story

Inspired by the legendary Ministry of Crab in Colombo, our digital outpost seeks to bring the same level of reverence to the data of the crab as the restaurant brings to the flesh.

This project was established to prove that machine learning models could predict biological age with sufficient accuracy to guide sustainable harvesting practices.

The Constitution

"We pledge to uphold the integrity of every data point, ensuring that no crab is misclassified, and that every age prediction serves the greater good of marine sustainability."
