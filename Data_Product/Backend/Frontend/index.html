The Declaration

The Ministry of Age was created in 2026 as a scientific homage to nature's most prized crustaceans. We believe that understanding the lifecycle of the crab is essential to appreciating its culinary value.

Our "Constitution of Crab" mandates that no guesswork shall enter the kitchen. Utilizing advanced machine learning algorithms trained on over 3,800 specimens, we determine age with unprecedented precision.

"Size is not just a number. It is a story of time."
